Privacy Policy for BudgetPro Navigator

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

1. Overview

BudgetPro Navigator is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the desktop application.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. BudgetPro Navigator does not:

  • Send data to external servers
  • Upload information to the cloud
  • Transmit financial data over the internet
  • Share information with third parties

3. Data Collection

We collect NO data. The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any user information outside of your local device.

4. Data Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data remains on your device in encrypted form.
  • No Internet Dependency: The App functions completely offline.
  • No Third-Party Access: No external services or APIs have access to your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use NO third-party services. The App does not include analytics tools, advertising networks, or connect to social media.

6. Compliance

BudgetPro Navigator complies with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and Windows Store Content Policies.
